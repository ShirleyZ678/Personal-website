Emotion = a state associated with stimuli that are rewarding (i.e., that one works to obtain) or punishing (i.e., that one works to avoid)

Emotions have an hedonic value, i.e. they are subjectively liked or disliked. Emotions have a particular ‘feeling state’ in terms of an internal bodily response (e.g. sweating, heart rate, hormone secretion). Emotions elicit particular external motor outcomes in the face and body (expressions). Emotions trigger action responses (e.g. fight-or-flight) and cognitive responses (e.g. increased attention)
