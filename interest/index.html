I was curious about psychology since I found human's behaviors are interesting and somehow unpredictable. So I want to learn about the conditions under which thoughts, feelings, and behaviors occur and how these variables influence social interactions. For my minor psychology, I took a class named social psychology which mainly discusses the psychological mechanisms behind human behavior and interpersonal relationship.

Social psychology is the scientific study of how the thoughts, feelings, and behaviors of individuals are influenced by the actual, imagined, and implied presence of others, 'imagined' and 'implied presences' referring to the internalized social norms that humans are influenced by even when alone.
